Luminous is a digital art installation by Orian Sharoni, Ronnny Ziss & Roee Shenberg.
Luminous invites you to participate in a multi-user ephemeral graffiti arena.
The installation was created using HTML5+Javascript+Node.JS. For any questions, contact Orian Sharoni, Ronny Ziss or Roee Shenberg on Facebook, or call Ronny @ 054-6462442
